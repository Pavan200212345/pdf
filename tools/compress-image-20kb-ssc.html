How to Compress Image to Exact KB Size

This free online image compressor helps you reduce image file size to an exact KB value. Whether you need to compress a photo to 20KB for SSC form, 50KB for UPSC application, or 100KB for banking exam registration, our tool delivers precise results.

Unlike other compressors that give approximate sizes, our tool uses an iterative compression algorithm. It adjusts the image quality step by step until the file reaches your exact target size. This ensures your photo meets the strict file size requirements of government job applications.

The compression happens entirely in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and faster processing. You can even use this tool offline once the page is loaded.

Supported formats include JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WebP. For best results, start with a high-quality image. The tool will automatically optimize the quality while maintaining acceptable visual clarity for document submissions.
