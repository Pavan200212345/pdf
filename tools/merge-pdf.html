How to Merge PDF Files

This free PDF merger tool allows you to combine multiple PDF documents into a single file. Perfect for compiling application documents, combining certificates, or creating document packages for submission.

Simply drag and drop your PDF files or click to browse. You can select multiple PDFs at once. After selecting, use the arrow buttons to reorder the documents in your preferred sequence.

The merged PDF maintains the original quality of all documents. Page sizes and orientations are preserved from the source files.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your documents are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security for sensitive documents.
