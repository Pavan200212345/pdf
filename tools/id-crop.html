How to Crop ID Card Images

This ID card cropping tool helps you precisely crop images of your Aadhaar card, PAN card, Voter ID, Driving License, and other government documents for form submissions.

Upload your ID card image and use the cropping handles to select the exact area you want to keep. You can choose from preset aspect ratios that match common ID card dimensions, or use free-form cropping.

Use the rotation buttons to correct the orientation if your scan or photo is tilted. The flip buttons help if your image is mirrored.

The cropped image is downloaded in high quality, suitable for uploading to government portals and application forms. All processing happens in your browser for complete privacy.
