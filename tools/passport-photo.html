How to Create Passport Size Photos

This passport photo tool helps you create perfectly sized photos for Indian government applications. Whether you need photos for SSC, UPSC, Banking exams, Aadhaar card, PAN card, or passport applications, our tool ensures your photo meets all requirements.

The standard passport photo size in India is 3.5 x 4.5 cm with a white background. Our tool automatically adjusts your photo to this size while maintaining proper proportions. You can also choose other common sizes like square format for certain applications.

For government forms, a plain white or light-colored background is typically required. Our tool adds a clean white background to your photo, making it suitable for official document submissions.

You can optionally add your name and date to the photo, which is required for some examination forms. The text is added at the bottom of the photo in a clear, readable format.
