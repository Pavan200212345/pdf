Why Use Govt Document Utility Hub?

Government job applications in India require strict image and document formats. Whether you're applying for SSC CGL, UPSC Civil Services, IBPS Banking exams, or updating your Aadhaar and PAN card, your files must meet exact size requirements.

Our tools help you compress images to exact sizes like 20KB, 50KB, and 100KB, resize passport photos, and convert documents quickly. Unlike many tools, we focus on precision and simplicity.

All processing happens directly in your browser — no files are uploaded. This ensures:

  • Complete privacy
  • Instant processing speed
  • No data storage or risk

You can also use tools like: Image to PDF, PDF Merge, Signature Resize, and ID Card Crop to prepare documents easily for any government form submission.

This platform is designed specifically for Indian users to solve real-world problems quickly — without complicated steps or unnecessary features.
