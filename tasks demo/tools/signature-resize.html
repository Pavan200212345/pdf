How to Resize Signature for Government Forms

This signature resize tool helps you compress signature images to exact KB sizes required by government job applications. Most SSC, UPSC, and Banking exam forms require signatures between 10KB to 20KB.

Upload your signature image and select the target size. Our tool uses intelligent compression to reduce file size while maintaining signature clarity and readability.

You can also specify exact pixel dimensions if the form requires specific width and height. Common requirements include 140×60 pixels for many government forms.

For best results, use a signature on white background with good contrast. Signatures with thin strokes may lose some clarity at very small file sizes.
